## Instructions

This assessment is designed to help you evaluate your organization's readiness for the agentic economy. For each question, select the answer that best describes your current state. Each answer has a point value. At the end of the assessment, total your points to determine your overall readiness score.

## Scoring

*   **0-12 Points: Nascent.** Your organization is at the very beginning of its agent-readiness journey. Immediate action is required to avoid being left behind.
*   **13-24 Points: Developing.** You have taken some initial steps, but a more structured and comprehensive approach is needed. Focus on building a solid foundation.
*   **25-36 Points: Advancing.** You are well on your way to becoming agent-ready. Focus on scaling your efforts and refining your strategy.
*   **37-39 Points: Leader.** Your organization is at the forefront of agentic commerce readiness. Focus on continuous improvement and staying ahead of the curve.
